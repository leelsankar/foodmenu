Add to Home Screen

Keeps your data safe — even when Safari restarts

Safari can wipe website data when you close the browser. Once installed as an app, your meal plans, pantry and settings are permanently stored and never cleared.

📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

1 In Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ⬆
Found at the bottom centre of the screen on iPhone, or top bar on iPad
2 Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3 Keep the name as-is and tap "Add" in the top-right corner
4 A Gatherly icon now appears on your home screen — open it from there every time ✅

🤖 Android (Chrome)

1 Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right of Chrome
2 Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app"
3 Tap "Add" — done! ✅
